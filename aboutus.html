Join us

We train on Sundays from 10:30 – 12:30 at Falmer Sports Centre, Brighton University.

We have players with all sorts of disabilities and everyone is welcome. If you would like to join us we would love to hear from you. Whether you want to play for fun and make friends or to train to compete in the national leagues and competitions – or even if you want to volunteer to help us as an official, coach or referee!
